# 🎯 HR Survey Analysis

## 📊 Project Overview

This project analyzes employee engagement survey data to uncover actionable insights on satisfaction, workplace culture, and retention drivers.

The objective is to transform raw survey responses into clear, business-oriented insights that support HR decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Project Goals

The aim was to uncover employee sentiment across 10 engagement dimensions, including:

* Recognition
* Purpose
* Supervision

The goal was to deliver clean, visual insights to help improve morale, inclusion, and overall performance culture.

---

## 📌 Business Needs

The HR team required:

* A reliable analysis of engagement survey results
* Cleaned and structured data for accurate reporting
* Visual tools to quickly identify strengths and improvement areas
* Actionable recommendations to enhance employee experience

---

## 📊 Data Overview

* **Tool:** Excel
* **Records:** 14,725
* **Fields:** 10 survey questions (ratings from 1–4)
* **Scale:** Likert scale

  * 1 = Strongly Disagree
  * 4 = Strongly Agree

---

## 🧹 Data Cleaning & Preparation

### Data Quality Checks

* Assessed min, max, count, and missing values
* Removed rows with blanks or duplicates
* Standardized inconsistent text fields

### Data Transformation

* Created a dedicated **Chart Source** tab
* Calculated:

  * Response counts
  * Average scores (excluding 0 values)
* Converted counts into percentages
* Sorted questions by descending average score

---

## ✅ Key Insights

### 👍 Strengths

* **Clear expectations (Q1):** 92% positive
* **Supportive supervisors (Q4)** and **job purpose (Q5):** ~85% positive
* Strong results in **daily work alignment (Q2)** and **diversity support (Q9)**

### ⚠️ Areas for Improvement

* **Recognition (Q3):** Only 29% strongly agree
* **Workplace relationships (Q6):** Very low engagement
* **Growth opportunities (Q7)** and **accountability (Q8):** moderate scores

---

## 💡 Recommendations

* Implement a **formal recognition program** (weekly/monthly)
* Encourage **social engagement** (events, buddy systems)
* Train managers on **feedback and accountability**
* Promote **career development & internal mobility**
* Continue strengthening **diversity initiatives**

---

## 🚀 Business Impact

This analysis enables HR teams to:

* Identify key engagement drivers
* Prioritize high-impact improvements
* Support data-driven people strategies
